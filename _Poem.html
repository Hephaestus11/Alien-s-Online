POEM

E:

Eager soul within, a journey's start, Exploring the depths of the beating heart. Echoes of laughter and shadows of pain, Every chapter written, a self to gain.

R:

Rays of affection, like sunlight above, Resilient bond, an unbreakable glove. Reveling in the warmth of an embrace, Riding the waves of love's tender grace.

O:

Obstacles may rise like mountains tall, Over them, we climb, never to fall. Optimism fuels our inner flame, Onward we march, unyielding in aim.

L:

Lifting the sails on a sea of dreams, Learning the beauty of patient seams. Life unfolds in its own sweet time, Like a patient poet weaving rhyme.
